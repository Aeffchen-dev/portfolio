Douglas 2023

Aligning the Douglas app and webshop UI with the premium assortment

Douglas strengthened its positioning as an upscale cosmetics retailer through comprehensive interface modernization. The previous design featured excessive visual complexity and color saturation, undermining brand coherence and diminishing perceived exclusivity.

As Teamlead UI/UX at Thalia Digital Retail Solutions, I led implementation of the DEPT design draft — mapping visual direction onto the existing design system while testing UI improvements to protect the established user experience.

The redesign prioritized enhanced brand recognition and premium appeal, improved omnichannel service opportunities, and an emotionally engaging beauty experience.

Accessibility compliance with the European Accessibility Act 2025 was a core requirement, shaping contrast decisions, interaction patterns, and semantic structure throughout.

Implementation required careful mapping of the new visual language onto hundreds of existing components — balancing the agency's creative direction with the technical constraints of the live product.

The redesign delivered a cohesive, accessible digital experience across app and webshop — elevating the brand's premium positioning while maintaining the usability of the existing platform.

Year:2023
Agency:Thalia Digital Retail Solutions
Brand:Douglas
Position:Teamlead UI/UX

I lead the implementation of the Dept design draft on a Design System Implementation and mapping side. I applied Dept's visual vision to the current feature set and tested loads of UI improvements and made sure nothing harms the UX in the end.
