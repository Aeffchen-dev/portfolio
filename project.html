Edition

Aligning the Douglas app and webshop UI with the premium assortment

The redesign prioritized enhanced brand recognition and premium appeal, improved omnichannel service opportunities, and an emotionally engaging beauty experience.

Accessibility compliance with the European Accessibility Act 2025 was a core requirement, shaping contrast decisions, interaction patterns, and semantic structure throughout.

Implementation required careful mapping of the new visual language onto hundreds of existing components — balancing the agency's creative direction with the technical constraints of the live product.

The redesign delivered a cohesive, accessible digital experience across app and webshop — elevating the brand's premium positioning while maintaining the usability of the existing platform.
